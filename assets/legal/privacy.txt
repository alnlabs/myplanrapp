MyPlanr — Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview
MyPlanr ("we", "ALNLabs") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

2. Information we collect
- Account: email, display name, authentication data
- Household: family roster, inventory, expenses, recipes, plans, shopping lists, subscriptions, and optional profile details you enter (including health notes if you choose to add them)
- Device: push notification tokens and local app preferences (theme, notification settings)
- Diagnostics: optional in-app logs you can view on your device

3. How we use information
We use your data to operate the app, sync household content, send reminders you enable, and improve reliability and security.

4. Storage and security
Data is stored with Supabase (hosted infrastructure). Photos and attachments are stored in private buckets accessible only to your household via signed URLs. We use industry-standard authentication and row-level security.

5. Sharing
We do not sell your personal information. Household data is visible to members of your household according to app permissions and visibility settings you configure.

6. Your choices
- Edit or delete profile information in the app
- Control notification permissions on your device
- Delete your account from Settings (permanent)

7. Data retention
When you delete your account, we remove your authentication record. Household content you created may remain for other members until they delete it.

8. Children
MyPlanr is intended for household use by adults. Parents or guardians manage profiles for children in the family roster.

9. Changes
We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact
Privacy questions: privacy@alnlabs.com
